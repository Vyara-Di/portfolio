Winery Reverto

Client
Reverto Winery
Role
Brand Designer
Services
Brand Identity, Label Design, Wordmark development
Intro

Reverto Winery is a fine wine brand focused primarily on international export markets.

(01)OverviewBrand Identity

The project began with exploring the language of wine labels and classical typography, evolving into a complete identity, logo, and packaging system. I designed three distinct label lines — classic, premium, and summer special—each with its own character while connected through a unified visual approach. Custom refinements to the Migha typeface led to the creation of a bespoke logo that defines the brand’s refined and contemporary identity.

(02)Classic selectionWordmark, Label design

This project set out to create a clean, minimal identity that reflects the wine’s origin and character.

01

Inspired by the structure of the typeface, typography became the foundation of the entire visual system. A restrained white background established clarity and balance, while the letter “V” emerged as the central design element—shaping the identity around a single, recognizable form.

02

Within the “V,” a local coastal landscape was integrated to highlight the region of production, with the bottling year reinforcing a sense of authenticity. Each wine variety was assigned a subtle color accent, creating distinction across the range while preserving a cohesive and unified collection identity.

(03)Premium selectionWordmark, Label design

This premium wine collection was designed to express exclusivity and refined sophistication through a bold, minimal visual language. A deep black background establishes a strong sense of elegance, enhanced by Art Deco–inspired geometry and subtle decorative detailing. Metallic gradients, drawn from precious materials, add depth and a tactile sense of luxury, reinforcing the collection’s high-end character at first glance.

(04)Summer special selectionWordmark, Label design

This limited summer-edition fruit wine collection was designed with a bold, vibrant visual identity to stand out on shelf and capture immediate attention. Playful fruit illustrations and saturated, flavor-driven color palettes express the fresh character of the range—orange for pear, purple for grape, and red for strawberry. The result is a cohesive yet distinctive system that highlights each flavor’s individuality while maintaining a unified collection identity.

Crafted to be poured. Designed to be remembered.
